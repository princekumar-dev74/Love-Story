My dearest Riya,

I've been trying to find the right words for a while now, and I've finally accepted that there aren't any perfect ones. So here are my imperfect, completely honest ones instead:

Thank you. For the laughter that makes my stomach hurt. For the way you listen — really listen — like what I say actually matters. For being the person I want to call first when something good happens, and the only one I want near when something doesn't.

You make ordinary days feel like something worth remembering. A Tuesday coffee, a ridiculous argument about which route to take, watching something we've both already seen — all of it is better because you're there.

I love you more than I know how to say. But I'll spend as long as you'll let me trying.

Yours, always — Arjun
